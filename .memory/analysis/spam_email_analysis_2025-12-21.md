### üö© Red Flag #5: The Split Offer
```

**This Is The Hook:**

**Why They Use 70/30:**

### üö© Red Flag #6: The Urgency Tactics

**Psychological Manipulation:**

- "keep this deal confidential" = isolation tactic

### üö© Red Flag #7: Grammar and Phrasing
```
"I have very vital information to give to you"
"in order words" (should be "in other words")

**Issues:**
- Formal but awkward English (common in scam templates)

### üö© Red Flag #8: The Personal Email Request
