## The Plant: Crane Clean Energy Center

**Location**: Susquehanna River, Pennsylvania  
**Original Name**: Three Mile Island Unit 1  
**History**: Operated safely 1974-2019, closed for economic reasons  
**New Name**: Crane Clean Energy Center (distancing from 1979 Unit 2 accident)

**Technical Specs**:
- **Type**: Pressurized Water Reactor (PWR)
- **Capacity**: 835 MW
- **Output**: Baseload (24/7 at constant power)
- **Capacity Factor**: Expected >90%
- **Fuel**: Low-enriched uranium
