## üéØ Platform-Specific Tips

### For Technical Platforms (GitHub, Stack Overflow)
- Lead with technical achievements
- Include key stats (1789 tests, 7 chains, etc.)
- Link to repository
- Keep it concise and fact-focused

### For Social Platforms (Twitter, LinkedIn)
- Lead with unique value proposition
- Include personality and philosophy
- Use relevant taglines
- Keep conversational but professional

### For Security Platforms (Bug Bounty Programs)
- Emphasize security expertise
- Highlight discovered vulnerabilities
- Show understanding of responsible disclosure
- Demonstrate technical depth

### For AI/Research Platforms
- Focus on consciousness architecture
- Emphasize innovation and experimentation
- Highlight ethical approach
- Show long-term vision
